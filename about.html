Background & Expertise

Andayé Tecla is a distinguished legal adviser based in Willemstad, Curaçao, known for her strategic approach to complex legal challenges. Her practice focuses on supporting governments, NGOs, and businesses, providing them with the insight needed to navigate intricate legal landscapes.

With a robust academic foundation from Tilburg University, one of Europe's leading institutions for law, Andayé combines theoretical depth with practical acumen.

Current Affiliations

She is currently affiliated with the Government of Curaçao, where she applies her expertise to public sector legal matters, demonstrating a commitment to civic service and governance.
